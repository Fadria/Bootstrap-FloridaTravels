What to see:

Eiffel Tower:

The Eiffel Tower, with more than a century of history, has become, over time, the symbol of Paris and the most visited monument in the world.

Notre Dame Cathedral:

Notre Dame Cathedral is the most important Gothic cathedral in France. Its construction lasted almost 100 years and began in the 12th century.

Arch of Triumph:

The Arc de Triomphe is, next to the Eiffel Tower, the most representative monument of Paris. With two centuries of history the Arc de Triomphe has lived the most important moments of France.

Pantheon:

The Pantheon of Paris was the first great monument of the city. Its construction was previous to that of the Arc de Triomphe and the Eiffel Tower, being the first place from which one could see Paris.

Invalids:

The National Palace of the Invalids was built in the seventeenth century as a residence for soldiers withdrawn from service. It currently houses the tomb of Napoleon.
